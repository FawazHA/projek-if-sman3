Pressure.

Pressure is a ROBLOX survival horror game developed by Urbanshade: Hadal Division. The creator and lead developer of Pressure is Zeal, also known as @YourFriendZeal on Roblox, Twitter/X and Discord. Pressure is the universe where URBANSHADE lies.

Gameplay

Overview

In Pressure, you play as a prisoner offered a deal by Urbanshade; a job in exchange for your freedom. Upon your agreeance, you are taken to the Hadal Blacksite, a research division created by Urbanshade operating at the bottom of the ocean. You are tasked with securing any loose assets, such as documents, USB drives, and DNA vials, ultimately retrieving the main power source of the facility, the Crystal. During your expedition you traverse numerous difficult challenges, such as environmental hazards, monsters, and puzzles. You are also able to utilize dropped tools to aid your survival, such as Flashlights to light up dark rooms, Code Breachers to hack locked doors, and healing items to replenish your health.
