THE_P.AI.NTER

The p.AI.nter, also known as Z-779, is a sentient AI and a threat in Pressure, acting as one of the many enemies the player will encounter as they venture through the Hadal Blacksite. Having been coded by ███████ ███████████████, it was captured by Urbanshade employees due to the surprisingly high quality of the art it creates. After these employees were demoted to LR-P for the unapproved murder of the AI's creator, it has been forced to mine Roblux for Urbanshade, as they had no other use for it.
